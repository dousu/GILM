Model

Application

Procedure

  1. Explain a purpose of our research and the procedure to participants
  2. Let participants play the application to get used to it
  3. Give a chance to ask questions about the application
  4. Make a pair in participants, which is one teacher and one learner

Procedure

  1. Give a each pair a generation number
  2. Give 10 times of communication on the generation(One step is that a learner imitates parent's music and they switch roles.)
  3. Give a record of communication next generation

Features

Meaning Space

  • A whole scores, 300 kinds
  • The iteration constructs new meaning by human feeling and bias.
  • A learner must guess a meaning of a teacher's to imitate it because the learner could not always choise scores teacher selected.

Learning Bottleneck

  • A half of scores, 150 kinds
  • When a teacher and a learner make music, they are given 150 scores which is selected randomly.
  • The learner would not construct the same music as the teacher's one.

Learning(Modification)

  • 9 times on the generation and record of a previous generation
  • A teacher could guess the learner's cognition what he/she focused on from the imitation.
  • Participants could refer the previous record, when making and imitating music.

Hypothesis

  • Pitch structure(similar keys, similar pitches and so on)?
  • Melodic pattern structure(a form based on difference between pitches)?
  • Is it explained by music theory?
